In general background image is used insert image in the background.background-color is used to set color of background.

  • Background-repeat is to manage repetition of image in background.there are 4 values.i,e. repeat/no-repeat/repeat-x/repeat-y.
  • Background-size is used to adjust size of image in background. there are 3 values.i,e. cover/1200px/1000px.
  • Background-position is used to set position of image in background.there are 5 values.i,e. top-right/top-left/bottom-right/bottom-left/center
  • Background-attachment is used to fix or scroll the image in background.there are 2 values.i,e. fixed/scroll

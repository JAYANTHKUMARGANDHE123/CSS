Border and Properties


  • border-width:width of line in pixels.


  • border-style:pattern of line(solid/dashed/dotted/double/inset/offset).


  • border-color: color of line.


  • border-collapse: to remove excess lines of border(collapse).


  • border-radius:to apply/cut the edges of the border to form circular (pixels).


  • Shorthand-property:=>border:2px solid blue;

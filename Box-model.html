Box-Model in CSS

Margin:
The space between the content and sides of a web-page is known as MARGIN.
Margin can also be written as
Outerspace of an element.
Padding:
The space between content and border.
Padding can also be called as
Innerspace of an element.
Border:
The line which is outside of the content within the Outline.
Outline:
The line which is outside of border is Outline.
Outline-offset
The space between outline and border is known as Outline-Offset.

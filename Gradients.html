GRADIENTS

  • Linear-gradient: To apply multiple colors at a time in background linearly.


  • syntax:

  • background-image:linear-gradient(direction,col1,col2,col3.....,colN).



  • Radial-gradient: To apply multiple colors at a time in background circullarly.


  • syntax:

  • background-image:radial-gradient(direction,col1,col2,col3.....,colN).


  • Note-1: direction=>to right/to left/ to bottom/to top left/to top right/ to bottom left/ to bottom right


  • Note-2: col1,col2.........colN represents colors.

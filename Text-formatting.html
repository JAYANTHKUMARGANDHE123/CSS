Text-Formatting

To make some changes within the format/content of the webpage is known as Text-Formatting. they are as follows:

  • font-family: to change style of the text
  • font-size: to change size of text
  • font-weight: to change weight of text[700-900]
  • text-decoration: to add/remove line to text(overline/underline/line-through/none)
  • text-transform:to change case of text(uppercase/lowercase/Capitalised)
  • Word-spacing: to increase/decrease space between words(pixels)
  • letter-spacing:to increase/decrease space between letters& words
  • line-height: to increase/decrease height between the lines of a paragraph
  • text-shadow: to add shadow to text(pxpxcolor)
  • text-align: to change alignment of text
  • font-style:to change into italic text(italic)
  • text-indent:to add space before start of a paragh(pixels)
  • margin: to create space between content of a element(pixels)
